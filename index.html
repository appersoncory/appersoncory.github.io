About Me

Hello, and thank you for visiting my portfolio site! My name is Adam Tull, and I'm a software engineer with a passion for all things programming, technology, and how they can be used to improve our lives. My favorite part of coding is the puzzle element of problem solving, and getting to collaborate with other like-minded engineers. I have a strong background in customer service and support, and look forward to furthering skills in my journey to create things that help people, showcase my skills to help solve problems, and work with other developers, as I become a full-time SWE in the near future. Coding and learning about technology and computers has always been a hobby of mine, and I'm thrilled to be where I am today to practice my passion each day. I'm well-versed in C# and Python, but I'm equally eager to learn more languages, skills, frameworks, and technologies as I begin working. So far, I've worked on my open source project called War Room.
I've spent many years working in custom focused jobs, which has made me well equipped to handle interacting with clients and conversing with them to understand their needs and how we can work together.

When I'm not hunting down and solving bugs, I enjoy reading, miniatue model painting, and exploring nature. I have a fraternal twin brother, an older brother, and a younger sister who I am quite close to.
